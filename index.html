What is CommunityInsight.AI?

A generative AI platform that transforms public feedback, surveys, and civic data into insights that drive action. No data science required. Built for local orgs, school districts, and nonprofit teams.

🚫 The Problem

Local orgs are overwhelmed with raw data but lack the tools or capacity to interpret it — leading to missed funding and disengaged communities.

✅ Our Solution

We turn civic data into clear, actionable insights using GPT-4-turbo, LangChain, Chroma, and multilingual embeddings.

🧠 How It Works

📥 Ingest
Socials, surveys, civic portals
🧠 Embed
OpenAI / Hugging Face
📊 Vector DB
Chroma / Pinecone
💡 LLM Insights
LangChain, GPT-4-turbo

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Problem: Raw data overwhelms community orgs.
  • Solution: AI-powered dashboards — no data science needed.
  • Tech: GPT-4, Chroma, LangChain, Hugging Face
  • Ethics: Public-only data. Transparent. Multilingual support.
